Delicious Kabsa Recipe

Description

Kabsa is a traditional Arabian dish, known for its rich flavors and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Kabsa spice mix
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Cooking Instructions

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Soak in water for 30 minutes, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chicken pieces and sauté until browned on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent. Stir in the garlic, ginger, and Kabsa spice mix, cooking for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, cooking until softened. Return the chicken to the pot, add the chicken broth, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Remove the chicken and set aside. Add the soaked rice to the broth and bring to a simmer. Place the chicken back on top of the rice, cover, and cook on low heat for about 20 minutes or until the rice is tender and has absorbed the liquid.
  6. If using, sprinkle raisins on top and let sit for 5 minutes before serving.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander.

Serving Suggestions

Kabsa is best served with:

  • A fresh green salad
  • Cucumber and tomato yogurt salad
  • Spicy sauce or chutney
  • Pita bread or Arabic bread
